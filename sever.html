  • Comprehensive Eye Test

    • Optimax is dedicated to providing high-quality and professional eye care services to every valued customer. The top priority of our excellent optometrists is to perform a comprehensive eye examination service for you.

  • The importance of comprehensive eye test

    • Regular eye examinations include: medical history collection, visual acuity and prescription checking, external and internal eye health examination, eye health review and long-term management intervention plan.

    Regular comprehensive eye exams not only help you decide whether you need to wear glasses or contact lenses, but also help you detect and intervene in the early stages of most eye diseases, which is especially important for early prevention and treatment.

  • Eye Test Considerations

    • Optimax's comprehensive eye exam will take about 30 minutes. If you are visiting Optimax for the first time, please arrive 10 minutes early so that we can set up a new file for you. For other tests information, please call our store - we would love to help you out.

  • Eye Test Cost

    • Most of Optimax's routine comprehensive eye exams are covered by Medicare, so no additional cost to you.

    For Australian students and visitors who have private insurance, you may be able to claim back the cost of a comprehensive eye examination depending on the policy of the insurance company.

  • Myopia Control Service

    • As the increasing number of people is doing more intense visual tasks, many may face myopia issues at an early age and be troubled by the continuous deterioration in prescription. Therefore, early myopia prevention and control is particularly important. It can help slow down or stop the rapid changes in prescription, maintain your vision level, and promote eye health.

    In myopia management, it is particularly important to have an experienced optometrist to utilize advanced clinical equipment to develop a reasonable and effective diagnosis and treatment services for you.

    The optometrists Optimax employed not only have completed the professional study of Optometry in Australian education framework, but also have completed extra myopia management courses, and obtained the relevant vocational certificate. With years of professional myopia control experience, they are able to provide personalized diagnosis and treatment plans based on the actual situation and needs of different customers.

    In the meantime, Optimax always selects the highest standard of new equipment and keeps updating our diagnostic and treatment methods. For example, Optimax purchased professional biometry equipment to measure axial length for customers who need myopia prevention, so that customers can enjoy the best quality service at any time and have the best vision.

  • Other Eye Examination Service

    • Optimax not only offers comprehensive basic eye examination services for all customers, but also offers more detailed customized eye examination services to suit your needs.

    We provide customized eye test services including but not limited to: glaucoma examination, dry eye treatment, diabetic examination, OCT retinal diagnosis, visual field test, contact lens consultations.

    No matter what kind of eye problems you may have, you can rely on Optimax as we will be dedicated to deliver the best quality eye disease diagnosis and treatment services.
